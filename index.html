About MBS

MBS Mimo's Bookshop is a general
mainly secondhand bookshop in Southeast England.
The stock consists of around 25,000 secondhand books
priced to compete with internet book price and
around 200 books and maps about the local area,
mostly Ramsgate, Broadstairs and Margate.
Our publications, books about Ramsgate Margate
Broadstairs these are all available to buy
in our bookshop as well as online,
